David Worm



Vocal Percussionist

Review

The vocal percussionist, David Worm also keeps the group on track with his stellar beat boxer skills as well as his middle eastern approach to singing is another stellar musical performer. David was a former member of the former group WeBe3 along with Joey Blake and Rhiannon. His musical style never ceases to amaze me and every time I catch a newer video, David is trying out new things and sounding absolutely amazing. I can not picture the success of the musical group Gimme5 without David's presence.
