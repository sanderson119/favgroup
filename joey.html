Joey Blake


The bass vocalist who keeps the group on track.

Review

Joey Blake, who is an acting associate professor at Berklee College of Music has been involved with many projects along with Bobby McFerrin. Joey was a founding member of Voicestra, SoVo'So' and WeBe3, (now Gimme5).

I have also seen this gentleman live and listen to quite a bit of his content on YouTube.Another astounding member of Gimme5, Joey is responsible for keeping that improvised music on a certain track as a bass singer. He is a master of improvisation along with every other member of this musical group. While Joey is known as a base, under Bobby McFerrin's tutelage, he also has a wide range and hits the soprano notes in what seems like an effortless manner.


Amazing performance by Joey Blake and Bobby McFerrin. I was there!
